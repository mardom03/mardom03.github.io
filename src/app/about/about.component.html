As part of my responsibilities, I have strengthened my skills in web development, database management, REST APIs, server administration, and service-oriented architecture. I have a natural affinity for coding and diving into complex processes. Given my academic background in computer science and mathematics, combined with a lifelong personal interest in financial markets, I am actively exploring career opportunities in software development or quantitative analysis. I thrive on immersing myself in intricate projects and solving problems efficiently. I am excited to leverage my skills to contribute to the field and discover new opportunities throughout my professional journey.
